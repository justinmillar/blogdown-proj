Biography

Justin Millar is a graduate student (PhD) in Denis Valle’s lab at the University of Florida. His dissertation research is focused on developing novel models for understanding malaria epidemiology in order to promote data-driven decision making. Justin is also a co-manager of the UF R Meetups.
